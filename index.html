2. What is environmental injustice and environmental racism?

Environmental racism is a form of racism in which a race is forced to live in or near unsafe conditions that could later impact their health. Environmental Injustice is harm to the environment that creates a split between races/groups/communities due to the state of their environmental living conditions.
